# ShopIQ — Selling Points

## The Problem

Most kirana owners today manage their business with:
- A physical khata notebook (gets lost, torn, no backup)
- Manual WhatsApp messages for billing (slow, error-prone)
- Memory and rough mental math for stock (leads to stockouts)
- No profit visibility (don't know daily margins)

## The ShopIQ Solution

One app on a ₹8,000 Android phone replaces all of that.

## Pitch Lines

- "Bill bano 10 second mein" — Create any bill in under 10 seconds
- "Khata check karo 2 tap mein" — Check any customer's dues in 2 taps
- "Stock update karo 5 second mein" — Update stock in under 5 seconds
- "WhatsApp pe bill bhejo instantly" — One button sends the full bill on WhatsApp

## Price Justification

ShopIQ saves a kirana owner roughly:
- 30 minutes/day on billing and record keeping
- 1–2 stockout events per month (each costing ₹200–₹500 in lost sales)
- 2–3 disputes per month from incorrect khata entries

At ₹299/month, the app pays for itself in the first day of use.

## Target Customer Profile

- Age: 30–55
- Tech comfort: uses WhatsApp daily, knows how to install apps
- Device: Android, 2–4GB RAM, 32–64GB storage
- Business size: 50–500 transactions/day
- Language: Hindi primary, some English
- Pain point: paper khata getting out of control

## Demo Script

1. Open app — dashboard shows today's sales at a glance (10 seconds)
2. Tap Quick Bill → search "Atta" → add → tap Save Bill (15 seconds total)
3. Tap "Send on WhatsApp" → WhatsApp opens with full bill pre-typed (3 seconds)
4. Go to Khata → tap "Ramesh" → see full ledger + Add Payment (5 seconds)
5. Go to Inventory → show LOW badges → tap Add Stock (10 seconds)
6. Settings → switch to Hindi → UI updates instantly (3 seconds)

Total demo: under 2 minutes. Owner sees the value immediately.
